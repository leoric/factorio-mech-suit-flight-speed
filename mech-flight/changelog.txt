Version: 0.0.6
Date: 2026-07-12
  Changes:
    - Flight speed now mirrors hypothetical walking speed on the current tile symmetrically:
      bonus tiles (concrete) speed you up, penalty tiles (oil ocean) slow you down. Previously
      penalties were cancelled out instead of applied, which didn't match "fly at the speed you'd
      walk here."
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Version: 0.0.5
Date: 2026-07-12
  Bugfixes:
    - Replaced the runtime speed override. `character.character_flying_speed_modifier` and
      `character.flying_speed_modifier` do not exist anywhere in the Factorio API, so the previous
      write silently failed (or errored out and got the event handler disabled) and flight speed
      was never actually being adjusted. Reimplemented using `character.character_running_speed_modifier`
      together with `character.is_flying`, which are real, documented fields.
  Changes:
    - Updated to Factorio 2.1.
